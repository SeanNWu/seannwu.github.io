Sean Wu

My name is Sean Wu, and I am an undergraduate student at Pepperdine studying computer science and mathematics. At Pepperdine I am advised by Dr. Fabien Scalzo. My research interests are computer vision, 3D reconstruction, generative AI, and natural language processing. I am currently researching 2D to 3D reconstruction of cerebral angiograms, computer vision in glaucoma diagnostics, and enhancing open-sourced LLMs through imitation learning.

At UCLA I am advised by Dr. Ira B. Kurtz in natural language processing and Dr. Kouros Nouri Mahdavi in the Glaucoma Artificial Intelligence Lab (GAIL).
