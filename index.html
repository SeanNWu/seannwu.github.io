Sean Wu

I'm Sean Wu, an undergraduate student at Pepperdine University majoring in computer science and mathematics, mentored by Dr. Fabien Scalzo. My academic focus revolves around applying computer vision and natural language processing to address scientific and health-related challenges. Currently, I'm engaged in research on tasks such as 2D to 3D reconstruction of cerebral angiograms, computer vision applications in glaucoma diagnostics, and improving open-source large language models (LLMs) through imitation learning.

At UCLA I am advised by Dr. Ira B. Kurtz where we study LLMs in medicine, and Dr. Kouros Nouri Mahdavi in the Glaucoma AI Lab (GAIL).

Currently, I am a research assistant for Professor James Zou at Stanford's AI Laboratory (SAIL).
